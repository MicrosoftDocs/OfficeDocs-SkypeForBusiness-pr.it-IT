## <a name="what-is-adal"></a><span data-ttu-id="f34ee-105">Che cos'è ADAL?</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f34ee-105">What is ADAL?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f34ee-109">Un'ampia gamma di app che agiscono come client possono sfruttare l'autenticazione moderna per ottenere assistenza per accedere a risorse protette.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f34ee-109">A variety of apps that act as clients can leverage Modern Authentication for help in getting to secured resources.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f34ee-110">In Skype for Business Server questa tecnologia viene utilizzata tra i client locali e i server locali per fornire agli utenti un livello adeguato di autorizzazione per le risorse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f34ee-110">In Skype for Business Server, this technology is used between on-premises clients and on-premises servers in order to give users a proper level of authorization to resources.</span></span>
