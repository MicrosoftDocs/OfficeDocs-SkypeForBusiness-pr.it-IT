### <a name="move-to-teams-using-move-csuser"></a><span data-ttu-id="f1e19-130">Passare a Teams usando Move-CsUser</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f1e19-130">Move to Teams using Move-CsUser</span></span>
